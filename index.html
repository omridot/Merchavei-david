WHO ARE WE?














Merchavei David is a settlement in Israel which is loacted in the area of Judea and Samaria. our settlement is composed of a very diverse community of people, Baalei teshuva,people who were raised religious, as well as ultra-orthodox. We live together in harmony with a common goal of settling the Land of Israel, Land of milk and honey. In this incredible stretch of land, we decided to return to a simpler way of life, with the belief that this is our calling to settle the land in these areas, even if it's not always, entirely comfortable.

you are welcome to get to know us through our website, browse through our pictures and the information online.
